In a world where convenience and personalization are key, Artificial Intelligence (AI) has started to play a major role in how we take care of our physical and mental health. From smart workout plans to AI-powered mental wellness apps, technology is helping people become more aware, consistent.

Fitness apps now use AI to create personalized exercise plans. Based on a user's age, goal, fitness level, and progress, the system suggests daily routines. Some smart gym equipment uses AI to correct form during exercises, reducing injury risk. Others provide real-time feedback and count reps automatically. Virtual AI trainers are available anytime, offering support like a personal coach without needing to schedule a session.
